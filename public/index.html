Roadmap

Q4 2023 - Q1 2024: Inception and Development

- Establish the core development team and define the vision for E Cash.
- Conduct initial research and feasibility studies to ensure a solid foundation.
- Develop the initial smart contract and technology stack.

Q2 2024 - Q3 2024: Token Design and Whitepaper

- Create a comprehensive whitepaper outlining E Cash's purpose, technology, and use cases.
- Design the E Cash token (symbol, supply, distribution) and establish the governance model.
- Seek input and feedback from the community and industry experts.

Q4 2024 - Q2 2025: Public and Private Sale

- Launch private sale rounds to early investors and partners who align with the project's goals.
- Prepare for a public sale, conducting extensive marketing and community outreach.
- Distribute tokens to private and public sale participants.

Q3 2025 - Q2 2026: Exchange Listing and Integration

- Negotiate and secure listings on major cryptocurrency exchanges to increase accessibility.
- Develop partnerships and collaborations for integration into various payment platforms.
